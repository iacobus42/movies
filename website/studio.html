Reviews by Studio

Overview

This panel shows the performance of a user selected studio in different genres and over time. Select the studio, or studio partnership, of interest from the dropdown menu and the graph to the right will update with the correct data.

The "by genre" tab shows the relative performances of the studio's films in each genre. Each grey point is a single film's score and the solid black dot is the mean for that genre for the studio. The dot size reflects the number of films in that genre.

The "by time" tab shows the performance of that studio's films over time. Each grey point is a unique film and the larger black dots are the monthly mean rating. The size of the black dot reflects the number of release in that month. The blue line is a least squares estimate of the change in the studio's mean film rating over time.
