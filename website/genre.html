Reviews by Studio

Overview

This panel shows the performance of a user selected genre and over time. Select the genre of interest from the dropdown menu and the graph to the right will update with the correct data.

Each light grey point is a unique film's rating and the solid black line is the monthly mean critic score for films in that genre. The blue line is the least squares estimate of the change in the mean rating over time. The grey ribbon is the 95% confidence interval about the mean.
