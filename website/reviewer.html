Reviews by Studio

Overview

This panel shows the changes in a reviewer's typical scores by genre and over time. Select the reviewer of interest from the dropdown menu and the graph to the right will update to display the correct data.

The "by genre" tab shows fraction of that reviewer's reviews that were positive by genre.

The "by time" tab shows the changes in the reviwer's typical score over time. Each point is the fraction of reviews that were positive in a given month and the size reflects the number of reviews in that month. The thick blue line is the loess estimate of the mean and shows any variance in reviewer "harshness" over time.

The "compared to other reviewers" tab shows how the select reviewer's reviews compare to his or her peers. The point reflects whether the review was positive (1) or not (0) and the fraction of all reviews that were positive. The fraction of all reviews that were "fresh" can be thought of as the probability that a random review will be positive. The blue line is the reviewer's estimated fraction of positive reviews by the average review. The dashed line is the "expected fit" if the reviewer was exactly average in "harshness". A blue line below the dashed line reflects harsher than average reviews and a blue line above the dashed line reflects more forgiving reviews.
